ASSASSIN'S CREED

Assassin's Creed is an action-adventure stealth video game franchise published by Ubisoft. It depicts a fictional millennia-old struggle between the Assassins, who fight for peace with free will, and the Templars, who desire peace through order and control. The series features historical fiction, science fiction and characters, intertwined with real-world historical events and figures.
